## 推文 1（最稳）

You don't need better tools.

You need fewer decisions.

Most people feel stuck because:
– too many options
– no clear priority

Try this:

1. Cut options to 3
2. Define what matters now
3. Act in 10 minutes

This is how I stop overthinking.

## 推文 2（更有冲击）

You're not bad at decisions.

You're overloaded.

When everything feels important,
your brain freezes.

Use this:

– Reduce (cut to 3 options)
– Align (what matters now?)
– Act (small irreversible move)

10 minutes. Done.

It's friction.

Clarity doesn't come from more thinking.

Try this loop:

2. Align
3. Act
