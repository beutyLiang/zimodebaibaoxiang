# 治愈系列 · Flow 提示词（5 条 × 15 秒）

> 风格：低饱和冷调电影感 · 东方空镜 · 情绪文案 · 轻音乐
> 每条 = Flow 第 1 段（8秒）+ Extend 第 2 段（8秒）= 约 15 秒
> 文字和音乐在剪映中添加

---

## 🎬 治愈 #1 · 雨窗

### 文案（剪映加）
> 你不需要想清楚所有事。
> 你只需要，安静一下。

### 第 1 段 Flow 提示词
```
A 8-second vertical video (9:16). Extreme close-up of raindrops slowly sliding down a window glass, each drop catching and refracting soft warm light from inside. Behind the window, a blurred cozy room with a warm lamp glow. Desaturated cool blue-gray tones, shallow depth of field. Camera stays still, only the rain moves. Slow, meditative, intimate. Soft natural rain sound. Cinematic film grain. No text, no people.
```

### Extend 第 2 段提示词
```
Camera slowly pulls focus from the raindrops on the glass to reveal the blurred city lights outside the window, bokeh circles of warm orange and cool blue. A gentle glow from a candle reflects on the wet glass. Everything is soft, dreamy, and still. Same desaturated cool-tone color grading. Slow, peaceful, no movement except falling rain. Cinematic, healing, contemplative. No text, no people.
```

### 剪映操作
- 0-5秒：无文字，纯画面沉浸
- 5-10秒：`你不需要想清楚所有事。`（白色粗体无衬线，居中，淡入）
- 10-15秒：`你只需要，安静一下。`（淡入）
- 🎵 音乐：剪映搜索 **「雨天钢琴」** 或 **「Piano Rain」**，选节奏最慢的

## 🎬 治愈 #2 · 晨茶

### 文案（剪映加）
> 世界很快。
> 但你可以很慢。

### 第 1 段 Flow 提示词
```
A 8-second vertical video (9:16). Close-up of a ceramic tea cup on a wooden table. Hot tea is being slowly poured from a clay teapot, steam rising gracefully in soft morning sunlight streaming through a window. Golden light catches the steam particles. Shallow depth of field, blurred green plant in background. Desaturated warm-cool tones. Camera very slowly pushes in. Meditative, warm, healing. Natural pouring sound. Cinematic film grain. No text, no people visible.
```

### Extend 第 2 段提示词
```
Continuing same scene. The tea cup sits still on the wooden table, steam curling upward in golden morning light. Camera slowly tilts up following the steam, revealing a window with soft morning light and green plants on the windowsill, everything bathed in warm golden glow. A book lies open next to the cup. Dust particles float in the light beam. Still, peaceful, warm. Same color grading. No text, no people. Healing, contemplative atmosphere.
```

### 剪映操作
- 0-5秒：无文字，纯画面
- 5-8秒：`世界很快。`（白色粗体，居中，淡入）
- 8-15秒：`但你可以很慢。`（淡入）
- 🎵 音乐：剪映搜索 **「晨间轻音乐」** 或 **「Morning Calm Piano」**

## 🎬 治愈 #3 · 落叶

### 文案（剪映加）
> 放下的不是努力，
> 是那些不属于你的焦虑。

### 第 1 段 Flow 提示词
```
A 8-second vertical video (9:16). A single autumn leaf falls in extreme slow motion through soft diffused light. Background is a blurred forest with golden and green tones. The leaf tumbles gently, catching light as it rotates. Desaturated cool-warm tones, cinematic shallow depth of field. Camera follows the leaf slowly downward. Ethereal, peaceful, melancholic beauty. Soft ambient forest sound, distant birds. Film grain. No text, no people.
```

### Extend 第 2 段提示词
```
The leaf lands softly on the surface of perfectly still water, creating gentle circular ripples that expand slowly outward. Camera looks down from above at the leaf floating on dark water, surrounded by expanding ripple circles. Golden light reflects on the water surface. Everything moves slowly, peacefully. Desaturated tones, the leaf is the only warm color. Meditative, healing, letting go. Soft water ripple sound. Film grain. No text, no people.
```

### 剪映操作
- 0-6秒：无文字，看叶子飘落
- 6-10秒：`放下的不是努力，`（白色粗体，居中，淡入）
- 10-15秒：`是那些不属于你的焦虑。`（淡入）
- 🎵 音乐：剪映搜索 **「森林钢琴曲」** 或 **「Autumn Calm」**

```

Desaturated dark tones with warm golden highlights only from the flame. Camera stays very still. Intimate, quiet, contemplative. Soft crackling sound. Cinematic film grain. No text, no people.

Camera slowly pulls back to reveal the candle sitting on a small wooden shelf. Around it, blurred shapes of books, a small plant, and a ceramic cup. Warm intimate glow in cool dark surroundings. Meditative, healing, safe. Same color grading. No text, no people.

## 🎬 治愈 #5 · 竹林

### 文案（剪映加）
> 你走得慢，
> 不代表你走错了。

### 第 1 段 Flow 提示词
```
A 8-second vertical video (9:16). Looking up through tall bamboo trees in a misty forest. Early morning light filters through the bamboo leaves, creating rays of soft golden light through the mist. The bamboo sways very gently in a light breeze. Camera slowly tilts upward toward the light. Desaturated green-blue cool tones with warm golden light rays. Ethereal, ancient, peaceful. Soft wind in bamboo leaves sound, distant flowing water. Cinematic film grain. No text, no people.
```

### Extend 第 2 段提示词
```
Camera continues tilting up, now looking almost straight up through the bamboo canopy. Light breaks through in star-like patterns between the leaves. Mist drifts slowly through the bamboo trunks below. A single bamboo leaf detaches and floats down slowly through the light rays. Ancient, wise, eternal feeling. Same desaturated green-blue tones with golden light. Healing, grounding, connected to nature. Wind and distant water sound. Film grain. No text, no people.
```

### 剪映操作
- 0-5秒：无文字，纯竹林仰拍
- 5-9秒：`你走得慢，`（白色粗体，居中，淡入）
- 9-15秒：`不代表你走错了。`（淡入）
- 🎵 音乐：剪映搜索 **「竹林古琴」** 或 **「Bamboo Forest Guqin」**

## 📋 制作流程（每条视频）

| 步骤 | 工具 | 操作 |
|------|------|------|
| 1 | Flow | 粘贴第 1 段提示词 → 生成 8 秒视频 |
| 2 | Flow | 点 Add to Scene → SceneBuilder |
| 3 | Flow | 点 Extend → 粘贴第 2 段提示词 → 生成 |
| 4 | Flow | 导出/下载完整视频（约 16 秒） |
| 5 | 剪映 | 导入视频 → 裁剪到 15 秒 |
| 6 | 剪映 | 添加文字（按时间轴，粗体无衬线，居中，淡入淡出） |
| 7 | 剪映 | 添加音乐（搜索推荐的关键词） |
| 8 | 剪映 | 导出 → 发布到 YouTube Shorts + 抖音 |
